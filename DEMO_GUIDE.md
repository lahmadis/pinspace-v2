**Act 1: Landing Page** (30 seconds)
- Point out the modern, clean aesthetic
- "This is PinSpace - a spatial approach to architecture portfolio sharing"

**Act 2: Network Navigation** (1 minute)
- "Instead of a flat grid, work is organized as an interactive network"
- Click WIT bubble ‚Üí expands to years
- Click Year 3 Fall ‚Üí expands to studios
- "It's like a Russian doll - each level reveals the next"

**Act 3: The 3D Room** (2-3 minutes) ‚≠ê **THIS IS THE KEY FEATURE**
- Click "Urban Interventions" studio
- "Now we're IN the studio space - like a physical critique room"
- Drag to orbit: "Walk around the room"
- Scroll to zoom: "Get closer to boards"
- Hover over boards: "They highlight"
- "This solves the group grading problem - see all work at once in spatial context"
- "Much better than scrolling through a grid"

**Act 4: Board Detail** (1 minute)
- Click any board
- Shows full view with student info, project details
- "Click to examine individual work"
- "Can download, comment, share"
- Back button returns to 3D room

**Act 5: Key Features** (30 seconds)
- Point out keyboard shortcuts (ESC, R)
- Reset camera button
- Professional presentation quality
- "Foundation for live critique features"
